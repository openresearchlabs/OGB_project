1 Differential abundance analysis

Analysis information:

  • Method: maaslin3
  • Taxonomic level: species, genus, family, phylum
  • Group variable: diet
  • Formula used for interaction: ~ diet * duration + (1 | id)
  • Formula used across groups: ~ diet
  • Formula used within groups: ~ duration + (1 | id)
  • Correction: Benjamini-Hochberg FDR adjustment
  • Detection threshold: 0.1%
  • Prevalence threshold: 10%
